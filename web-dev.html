Website development process



1. Discovery & Research


The discovery and research phase begins with a detailed client consultation to understand their goals, target audience, and unique selling propositions. Market research follows, analyzing competitors and industry trends to identify opportunities. User research through surveys and interviews provide insights to target audience behaviours and needs, ensuring the website meets user expectations.



2. Planning & Strategy


Planning involves defining clear objectives and creating a detailed sitemap outlining the website's stracture. User personas and journeys guide design decisions, while a content strategy, including a content calender, ensures all necessary and optimized content is developed to engage users effectively.



3. Design Phase


The design phase starts with wireframes to establish layout and functionality, followed by high-fidelity mockups detailing design elements. Focused on UI/UX design, this phase ensures intuitive navigation and visual appeal aligned with the client's brand. Client feedback is continuously incorporated to refine designs.



4. Development Phase


Front-end development converts designs into responsive HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. Back-end developmet sets up server, database, and CMS, creating custom functionalities as needed. Rigorous testing ensures site is bug-free, secure, and performs well, providing a seamless user experience.



5. Content Creation & Integration


Content creation involves developing and gathering optimized text, images, and videos. This content is integrated into the website, ensuring proper formatong and consistency. The site is made informative and engaging, supporting its goals and senhancing user experience.



6. Launch & Optimization


A pre-launch checklist ensures all functionalities work as intended before the website goes live. Post-launch, the site is monitored for issues, with ongoing support provided. Continuous Optimization enhances performance and user experience, keeping the site effective and relevant.



7. Marketing & Promotion


SEO best practices improve search engine ranking, and analytics track performance. digital marketing strategies like social media, email campaigns, PPC drive traffic to the site. These strategies are monitored and adjusted based on performance dara to ensure effective promotion.



8. Continuous Improvement


Regular user feedback identifies areas for improvement. A/B testing optimizes design and content for better engagement. Updates and enhancements keep the site fresh, aligning with the latest trends and technological advances, ensuring it meets user needs and stands out.
